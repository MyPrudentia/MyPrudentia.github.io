Who We Are

Prudentia is one of the leading schools of its kind! Prudentia is a nonprofit organization that is run entirely by students and has the goal of providing students all around the world with access to a high-quality education in computer science and finance at no cost.Our goal is to give our students the skills, experience and confidence they need in order to become successful professionals. Our program is flexible and our teachers are the best in their field.
